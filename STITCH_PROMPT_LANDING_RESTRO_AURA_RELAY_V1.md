# EnergyOS Landing - Restro Aura Relay V1

Create a new standalone Stitch project for a public EnergyOS landing page.

The page must include every section below. Do not stop after the hero or diagnostic rail. Do not add the footer until after the modules, FAQ and final diagnostic CTA have been created.

## Non-negotiable direction

The base style is Restro.
Use Aura-inspired green color language.
Use the RelayEstate landing concept as conversion structure inspiration.

Do not make a generic Aura page. Do not make a generic SaaS page. Do not reuse the previous mixed Stitch project.

## Product

EnergyOS is an energy operations layer for professional tourist-apartment and serviced-apartment portfolios in Spain.

It combines:

- PMS/reservation calendars.
- Datadis, CUPS and electricity invoices.
- Tariff and contracted-power diagnostics.
- Intelligent control rules for connected appliances/devices.
- Solar and battery arbitrage analysis when assets exist.
- Owner-ready reporting.

The buyer is a professional accommodation manager. They want fewer hidden energy leaks, cleaner owner explanations and a credible pilot, not abstract sustainability messaging.

## Market positioning

Lead with the full platform spirit:

- Intelligent control of devices and appliances.
- Tariff and contracted-power optimization.
- Datadis/CUPS/invoice intelligence.
- Battery and solar arbitrage as an advanced module.
- Owner reporting and lead capture.

But keep claims prudent:

- No fake social proof.
- No guaranteed ROI.
- Do not say Datadis is real-time control.
- Datadis is diagnostic and consumption intelligence.
- Battery/arbitrage must be visible in the first viewport, but not the only concept.

## RelayEstate concept to adapt

Use RelayEstate as a structural reference, not as a real-estate copy clone:

- Premium, brand-forward landing page.
- Strong first-view diagnostic narrative.
- A vertical "leak rail" or timeline showing where value escapes.
- Alternation of dark cinematic sections and light editorial sections.
- Proof cards based on mechanisms, not unsupported metrics.
- FAQ / objections block.
- Strong final diagnostic/audit CTA.
- High-ticket service feel, not cheap template feel.

## Visual style

Base: Restro.

- Modular, premium, mid-century operational dashboard feeling.
- Warm, tactile surfaces and large rounded cards.
- Calm, architectural spacing.
- Generous whitespace.
- Compact data modules with clean alignment.
- Plus Jakarta Sans or similar geometric sans.
- Monospace only where needed for technical labels, prices and energy values.
- Cards may use 24px to 32px radius like Restro.
- Buttons/inputs around 10px to 12px radius.
- No purple gradients.
- No neon tech background.
- No decorative orbs.
- No glassmorphism as the main language.

Aura green color adaptation:

- Background should be green-toned, not beige/orange.
- Use soft green paper and sage tones instead of Restro beige.
- Primary ink: #102017 or #14261B.
- Deep forest: #1F3A2A.
- Surface: #F5F7F0, #FFFFFF, #E8EFE5.
- Border: #D2DED0.
- Primary accent: #7EA66A.
- Bright functional accent: #B7E86A, used sparingly.
- Secondary blue-green accent: #5DAA9B, only for data differentiation.
- Warning amber is allowed only for small alert states, not brand.

## Landing structure

Use Spanish copy only.

### Section 2 - Diagnostic rail

Headline:
"Donde hoy se pierde margen"

Create a RelayEstate-inspired vertical diagnostic rail with 5 stages:

1. Reserva: "El calendario dice que no hay huespedes."
2. Consumo: "La curva muestra gasto activo."
3. Tarifa: "La potencia contratada no encaja con el patron."
4. Bateria: "Hay horas para cargar, descargar o evitar punta."
5. Informe: "El propietario necesita una explicacion defendible."

### Section 3 - Platform loop

Headline:
"Detecta, decide, automatiza y reporta."

Four horizontal or bento steps:

- Detecta: "Cruza reservas, CUPS, facturas y curvas horarias."
- Decide: "Prioriza fugas, potencia, tramos y oportunidades de arbitraje."
- Automatiza: "Activa reglas para aparatos conectados cuando existe integracion."
- Reporta: "Convierte decisiones tecnicas en informes claros para propietarios."

### Section 4 - Modules

Headline:
"Una capa para toda la operacion energetica"

Six Restro cards:

- Control inteligente de aparatos
- Datadis, CUPS y facturas
- Optimizacion de tarifa y potencia
- Arbitraje solar/bateria
- Alertas operativas
- Informes para propietarios

### Section 5 - Objections / FAQ

Use a premium accordion-like FAQ layout:

- "Necesito hardware para empezar?"
  "No. El diagnostico puede empezar con reservas, facturas, CUPS y datos de consumo. El control fisico llega despues."
- "Datadis controla los aparatos en tiempo real?"
  "No. Datadis aporta inteligencia de consumo y diagnostico. Las reglas de control usan integraciones con dispositivos cuando existen."
- "Esto sirve si no tengo bateria?"
  "Si. La optimizacion de tarifa, potencia, fugas e informes ya tiene valor. La bateria es modulo avanzado."
- "Que recibe una gestora en el piloto?"
  "Mapa de fugas, recomendaciones priorizadas, reglas iniciales y un informe defendible para propietarios."
