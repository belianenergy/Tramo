# Tramo — Product Context

## Register
product — Tramo is a B2B SaaS energy management platform for professional tourist rental portfolios.

## Target Users
- Professional property managers and landlords with 5+ apartments in Spain
- Real estate firms and holiday rental operators managing distributed portfolios
- Energy efficiency consultants auditing multi-property clients

## Product Purpose
Tramo connects to CUPS (electricity meters) via Datadis to track, analyse, and optimise energy consumption across distributed tourist apartments. It detects standby drain during vacant periods, applies pricing-optimised rules, generates owner reports, and supports sensor/battery investment decisions through simulation.

## Job to Be Done
Reduce energy cost per apartment 15–30 % without guest-facing disruption, using data the owner already has access to.

## Brand & Personality
Precise, operational, discreet. Tramo does not shout. Every data point, every green accent, every tight radius exists because it earned its place. The brand feels like the engineer who made the spreadsheet sing — competent, understated, quietly rigorous. No purple gradients, no Inter, no decorative hero phones.

## Anti-References
Over-designed AI startup landing pages, Inter font everywhere, purple-to-blue gradients, glassmorphism, decorative mockups, hero images of laptops holding charts, rounded-square icon tile above every heading.

## Visual Anchors
ReflexAI.com — B2B clean, metrics in the foreground, direct CTA, trust signals. Linear.app — precise, restrained, function-first. Refero/Sourceful — warm B2B energy editorial, data-rich with real charts.

## Strategic Principles
1. Measure before installing — data first, hardware second
2. No ROI guarantees — scenarios, simulation, prudent estimation
3. Profitable without subsidies — tariff optimisation beats solar capex
4. Battery/arbitrage is premium line, not hook
