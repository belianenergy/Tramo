# Stitch Prompt — Tramo V1

Create a complete web front concept for **Tramo**, a B2B energy operations platform for professional tourist-property managers in Spain.

Use this exact strategic hierarchy:

- 60% Sourceful-like energy infrastructure identity: cream/paper, ink black, one signal orange, short editorial headlines, physical energy data, fine technical diagrams.
- 25% Seline/Pirsch-like analytics clarity: clean cards, metrics, charts, real product panels, module separation.
- 15% Orderful/Precision B2B conversion rhythm: clear CTA structure, alternating text/product blocks, compact lead qualification, serious operational software.

Do not create a collage. Sourceful-like identity leads. Analytics makes the product legible. B2B conversion sets the page sequence.

## Product Story

Audience: Spanish tourist-apartment managers with 10+ units.

Pain:

1. Consumption outside reservation.
2. Tariff or contracted power mismatch.
3. Owners see unexplained energy bills.

Promise:

Tramo helps managers detect non-operational consumption, prepare operational rules for appliances around stays, review tariff/power, generate owner-ready energy reports and identify where batteries/arbitrage deserve analysis.

Battery/arbitrage is premium/future qualification, not the core promise.

Core wedge:

The product's core wedge is not generic energy management: it is explaining and acting on kWh by reservation window, idle period, tariff/power period and owner report. Every screen must reinforce this attribution layer.

Prioritize implementation feasibility: Stitch may show 8 coherent screens, but the commercial MVP is landing + portfolio dashboard + operations queue + owner report + qualification form. ## Landing Structure

Hero:

- Cream background.
- Tramo logo.
- H1 above.
- Subcopy above.
- CTA: "Diagnosticar mi cartera".
- Secondary CTA: "Ver dashboard demo".
- Right-side product panel, not a phone mockup, not a decorative render.

Problem:

- 3 pains only:
  - "Consumo fuera de reserva"
  - "Potencia y tarifa heredadas"
  - "Propietarios sin explicación clara"

System:

- Flow diagram: Reservas/PMS -> CUPS/Datadis + facturas -> decisiones -> informes.

Modules:

- Reglas operativas para aparatos.
- Tarifas/Potencia.
- Informes propietarios.
- Evaluación batería/arbitraje, solo si los datos lo justifican.

Product proof:

Conversion:

  - nombre,
  - empresa,
  - email,
  - units,
  - city/region,

## Hard Restrictions

- No generic green-energy look.
- No dark AI dashboard as main identity.
- Do not use Lumina Grid dark/green as a visual base. Only borrow monitoring density if useful.
- Do not copy Sourceful, Seline, Pirsch or Orderful literally. Make Tramo's segmented-interval mark and reservation-energy attribution the unique visual device.
- No "AI" as primary claim.
- No "ahorro garantizado".
- No "hasta 30%" claims.
- No climatization-only framing.
- No decorative orbs, blobs or gradients.
- No phone mockup hero.
- No text overflow.
- No horizontal scroll at mobile 375px.
- At mobile 375px and 390px, stack the hero cleanly: text first, product panel below, CTA visible, no squeezed title.
- Do not ask users to upload invoices or submit Datadis/CUPS credentials.
