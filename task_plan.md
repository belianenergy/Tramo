# Mission Plan — Tramo / EnergyOS Market Launch Front

## Goal

Prepare a market-ready front for the EnergyOS concept under a stronger brand direction, using a new Stitch project and a blueprint-driven handoff to Codex via `/goal`.

Target outcome for Mauro waking up: a commercially launchable front/demo for lead capture and sales conversations, not a fully production-hardened SaaS with real integrations.

## Scope

- New brand/name/logo direction to replace EnergyOS as public brand.
- Full blueprint covering product, UX, brand, pages, data model assumptions, QA, and Codex implementation requirements.
- New Stitch project with landing and all platform pages.
- Three gstack validation loops with cross-review between subagents.
- CEO final approval after loop 3.
- Codex `/goal` construction brief generated from the approved blueprint and Stitch output.

## Critical Rules

- Sourceful-like identity leads: cream/paper, ink, signal orange, physical energy data.
- Seline/Pirsch-like product clarity: charts, cards, metrics, clean module separation.
- Orderful/Precision-like conversion: strong CTA rhythm and compact qualification.
- No generic AI/mobile app aesthetic.
- No unsupported savings claims.
- No climatization-only positioning.
- EnergyOS remains internal project name; public brand is reworked.

## Errors / Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Stitch produces beautiful but generic screens | Use strict prompt and loop validation against blueprint |
| Scope explodes beyond overnight feasibility | Keep goal as launchable front/demo, not production SaaS |
| Brand name/logo weak | CEO loop has explicit kill/keep authority |
| Codex receives vague instructions | Build blueprint and `/goal` brief before delegation |
