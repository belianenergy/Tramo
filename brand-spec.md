# Tramo Brand Spec

## Tokens

```css
:root {
  --bg:      oklch(98.7% 0.014 48);
  --surface: oklch(99.5% 0.006 52);
  --fg:      oklch(22% 0.025 55);
  --muted:   oklch(45% 0.035 55);
```

## Typography

- Display: `"Iowan Old Style", "Charter", Georgia, serif`
- Body: `"Inter", system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", sans-serif`
- Mono: `"JetBrains Mono", ui-monospace, SFMono-Regular, Menlo, monospace`

## Layout Posture

- Warm cream canvas with restrained editorial grid, not glass panels or dark mode.
- Orange appears as a sparse operational signal: primary CTA, alert peaks, critical values.
- Teal is the secondary system color for telemetry, active sync and controlled states.
- Cards use real borders, small radii and no decorative glow.
- Product copy stays specific to Spanish tourist apartments, CUPS, Datadis, PMS and owner reporting.
